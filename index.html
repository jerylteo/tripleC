Who are we?

Founded in November 2019, based in Nanyang Polytechnic, Singapore. TripleC is inspired by the love and passion for fashion - Our vision is to give everyone a chance to express themselves through color, design and style.

We believe that any design is a good design; every single person has their unique tastes. We allow you to create your own sense of fashion and personality
